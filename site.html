Radiohead: A Musical Abomination

In a world where good music reigns, a discordant note echoes - the existence of Radiohead. Their songs are a cacophony of confusion, filled with the nonsensical ramblings of a lead singer who can't sing.

Their music bellowed through the airwaves, a chorus of despair for the ears that had to endure it. They sang of nothing, with lyrics as deep as a kiddie pool and melodies that made you question the meaning of life.

A particularly brave music lover, named Melodia, stepped forward to lead the protest against Radiohead's music. Her voice boomed, echoing the sentiments of the people who had suffered through their songs.

Meanwhile, in a nearby music venue, a music enthusiast named Harmonia watched the Radiohead fanbase with confusion. She wondered why they would willingly subject themselves to such aural torture.

Radiohead: The Abomination of the Music World

In a world where good music reigns, a toxic plague festers - the existence of Radiohead. Their songs are an aural affront, filled with the incoherent ramblings of a lead singer who butchered the very concept of melody.

Their music polluted the airwaves, a cacophony of misery for the innocent ears that had to endure it. They sang of nothing, with lyrics as shallow as a drainpipe and melodies that made you question the sanity of their fans.

A valiant warrior, named Melodia, rose up to lead the crusade against Radiohead's atrocities. Her voice thundered, echoing the outrage of the people who had suffered through their aural assaults.

Meanwhile, in a nearby music venue, a music enthusiast named Harmonia watched the Radiohead fanbase with utter disgust. She wondered why they would willingly subject themselves to such an aural abomination.
